# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should be respectful, constructive, and professional. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- assume good intent
- discuss ideas, not people
- give actionable technical feedback
- be respectful of different experience levels
- keep disagreements focused on the work

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or threats
- hateful or discriminatory language
- doxxing or sharing private information
- repeated disruptive behavior after warning

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately through the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
